Bowdoin-Dining-iOS

A Bowdoin Dining app for the next generation of Bow-diners. This app is the compliment of the Bowdoin Dining Chrome extension (code available here), and is intended to replace the outdated version currently on the iOS App Store. It includes all the features of the original in a cleaner, more modern design aesthetic, and adds some new ones like access to personal account information. I plan to release this app for iOS 8 in September.
